NMR-PISEMA Spectra of 1D 2D structure of protein

  • Summary of the project

    • Today, in the protein data base, we have already got a lot of structures of all kinds of proteins. However, most of them are soluble protein. Up till now, very little is known about the structure of membrane protein. As we know, membrane protein is very important because of their critical functions such as ion/molecular transport, communication and energy transduction. So structure biology of membrane proteins is of great interest.

    We use 15N to uniformly label this protein and reconstitute it into lipid bilayers. Solid state NMR PISEMA (Polarization Inversion Spin Exchange at Magic Angle) experiments and PISA (Polar Index Slant Angle) wheel analysis are run to determine the protein's structure through 15N-1H dipolar/15N chemical shift.
